Engineering at PBS

What brings you here?

Do you want to learn about the engineering team that delivers over 2,000,000 video streams a week? Are you interested in working on that team? Then you're at the right place.

Our Mission

PBS uses media to "educate, inspire, entertain and express a diversity of perspectives". At PBS Digital we try to make the world a better place.

PBS Digital engineers make products that deliver videos that have an impact on our audiences. Children are more prepared for school through PBS KIDS programs. Adults learn and enjoy our content spanning genres like:

  • Arts & Culture
  • Drama
  • Science & Nature
  • History
  • Independent Films
  • News & Public Affairs

We're the engineering team that delivers this life-changing media. We do it every day for people across the United States.

Engineering Precepts

We're a small team responsible for big projects. Given these constraints we've adopted some core principles:

  • Bias toward action
  • Collegiality
  • Curiosity

Bias toward action

PBS Digital engineers build products for people. Our motivation is to ship code to people with consideration to speed and stability. We are autonomous and often rely on self-determination to pass through ambiguity.

Collegiality

Like PBS' media means to "educate, inspire, entertain and express a diversity of perspectives" so do our engineers. These are qualities we foster in our interactions with each other. We create a place where people value psychological safety and encourage experimentation.

Curiosity

PBS engineers continuously pursue mastery of their craft. We work closely with their counterparts in product development. Our cross-functional teams include engineers, designers, and product managers.
